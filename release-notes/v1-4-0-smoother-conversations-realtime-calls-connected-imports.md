# v1.4.0 — Smoother Conversations, Realtime Calls & Connected Imports

Released: July 20, 2026 v1.4.0 makes AI conversations smoother, adds a focused Realtime voice experience, and gives imports and their connections a clearer workflow. What changed Smoother conversation

Canonical source: [https://dokki.one/pub/docs/v1-4-0-smoother-conversations-realtime-calls-connected-imports](https://dokki.one/pub/docs/v1-4-0-smoother-conversations-realtime-calls-connected-imports)

**Released:** July 20, 2026

v1.4.0 makes AI conversations smoother, adds a focused Realtime voice experience, and gives imports and their connections a clearer workflow.

## What changed

### Smoother conversations

- Starting from Home or the AI panel now follows a more consistent conversation path.
- Conversation lists distinguish loading, empty, and error states more clearly.
- Avatars, conversation state, and scroll position remain steadier while you move through the panel.

### Realtime voice

- Voice calls open in a focused call view with status, duration, transcript context, mute, and hang-up controls.
- The conversation remains available underneath the call, and transcript context stays available after hang-up.
- Leaving or ending a call closes microphone and audio use more reliably.

### Imports and connected apps

- Import connections have a dedicated management area in Connected Apps.
- Provider setup uses clear fields instead of a raw configuration box.
- The import flow works better on smaller screens, keeps selections when switching sources, and confirms broad imports.

### Publishing and search freshness

- Published document translations are reused more reliably after resources with embeds are updated.
- New and updated documents, tables, and files become searchable sooner during busy periods.

## Try it

- Start a conversation from Home, then continue it from the AI panel.
- Start a Realtime voice call and review the transcript after hanging up.
- Import content on a narrow screen and open the created resource from the results view.

## Do I need to do anything?

No action is required. If an external app uses a custom OAuth application, an administrator may need to complete the provider-specific setup shown by Dokki.
