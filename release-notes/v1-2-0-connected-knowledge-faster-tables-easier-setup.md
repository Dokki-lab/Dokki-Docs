# v1.2.0 — Connected Knowledge, Faster Tables & Easier Setup

Canonical source: [https://dokki.one/pub/docs/v1-2-0-connected-knowledge-faster-tables-easier-setup](https://dokki.one/pub/docs/v1-2-0-connected-knowledge-faster-tables-easier-setup)

**Released:** July 18, 2026

v1.2.0 makes workspace knowledge easier to connect and find, improves table reliability, and makes account setup and AI model choices clearer.

## What changed

### Connected knowledge and search

- Resource Links can connect related documents across workspaces without creating another copy.
- Deep Search can use a broader mix of documents, tables, files, and related workspace knowledge.
- Published sites have improved home-page structure, cover handling, and search metadata.

### Tables

- Multi-step table changes are handled more reliably.
- Large tables and repeated edits have clearer limits and more predictable results.
- Table calculations and summaries can be completed with fewer manual steps.

### Account and AI setup

- Signup, email confirmation, invitations, and returning to the correct page recover more clearly when a step is interrupted.
- AI models are grouped more consistently by Free, Pro, and Max availability.

## Try it

- Link a project brief to related research in another workspace.
- Ask Deep Search a question that needs information from both documents and tables.
- Review an important table after a large multi-row update.

## Do I need to do anything?

No action is required for existing workspaces. If a previous signup or invitation was interrupted, restart from the latest email or sign-in page.
