## Workspace membership

A workspace has members with roles. Admins manage settings and members. Editors create and edit resources. Viewers can read content they are allowed to see.

## Resource permissions

Individual resources can have direct roles. A direct resource role can give someone access to a private resource, or cap access when the resource should be more restrictive than the surrounding workspace.

## General access

General access can be private, workspace-wide, or link-based. Link access can allow viewing, commenting, or editing depending on the selected level.

## Organization workspaces

Organization workspaces sit under an organization account context. Organization policies can control who creates workspaces, which models are allowed, spending limits, storage, and billing.

## Archived workspaces

Archived workspaces are hidden from normal workspace lists and excluded from background activity. Restore them when you need to work there again.
