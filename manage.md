## In this section

- **Version History** - review or restore earlier document states.
- **Export & Import** - move content in and out of Dokki.
- **Trash & Restore** - recover deleted resources.
- **Notifications** - handle mentions, access requests, agent attention, and system updates.
- **Tags & Pins** - classify resources and keep shared priorities visible.

## Day-to-day organization

Use folders for hierarchy, tags for cross-cutting classification, and pins for the small set of resources everyone should find quickly. Archive a workspace when an entire project is inactive; archive does not replace trash, and it is not a substitute for private-resource access control.

Organization admins also have organization-level settings for workspace creation policy, AI controls, billing, and model access.
