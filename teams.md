## Workspace Team billing

Workspace Team billing applies to one workspace. It is separate from organization billing and separate from a user's personal Pro or Max plan.

A Free workspace currently allows up to 20 writer/admin collaborators beyond the owner. Viewers are unlimited and do not consume the cap. The owner is never counted.

## Sponsored seats

A sponsored seat is a writer or admin in the workspace who is on the Free user plan and is not the workspace creator. Pro, Max 5x, and Max 20x users bring their own license and do not consume a sponsored seat.

## Team plan seats and price

The Team workspace plan includes 10 sponsored seats in the $20/month base price. Additional sponsored seats cost $2 per seat per month. Team workspace capacity is based on the purchased total seat budget.

Use the workspace billing screen as the source of truth for the current workspace plan, sponsored-seat count, seat budget, scheduled changes, and Stripe renewal state.

## Collaboration limits

Free workspace limits are intentionally smaller than Team workspaces. Upgrade a workspace when you need more Free-plan editors/admins in that workspace. Upgrade individual users to Pro or Max when those people need their own storage, AI quota, and paid-model access across workspaces.

## Choosing between Pro, Team, and organization billing

Use Pro or Max when one person needs more AI, storage, and model access. Use workspace Team when one workspace needs more sponsored collaborators. Use organization billing when the company needs shared member management, organization workspaces, model controls, spending limits, audit access, and shared AI balance.
