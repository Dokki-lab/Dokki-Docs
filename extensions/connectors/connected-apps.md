Canonical source: [https://dokki.one/pub/docs/connected-apps](https://dokki.one/pub/docs/connected-apps)

Use **Connect** to manage how Dokki works with external AI clients, apps, and messaging channels.

## Choose the right connection

- **AI Clients** — let an external AI client access the Dokki resources you authorize.
- **MCP Apps** — let Dokki use an external service, such as a content, communication, or productivity app.
- **Channels** — let you message the built-in Dokki Agent from a supported chat service.

## Connect an app

- Open **Connect → MCP Apps**.
- Find the app you want to use and choose **Connect**.
- Complete the sign-in or credential steps shown for that app.
- Review the account and permissions before finishing.

## Use a connected app

After the connection is active, ask Copilot or an agent to use the app for a specific task. Dokki may ask you to confirm an action before it changes or sends information in the external service.

## Recent AI-client activity

Open **Connect → AI Clients** to see which clients have used Dokki recently. Recent activity helps identify active clients, but it does not replace the connection's current permission or sign-in status.

## Disconnect or reconnect

Disconnect an account when it is no longer needed or belongs to the wrong person. Reconnect when the provider reports an expired or invalid sign-in. Disconnecting stops future use but does not delete content that was already imported or created.

## If a connection does not work

- Confirm that the connection shows as active.
- Check that you connected the intended external account.
- Review the workspace and resource access used by the current conversation.
- Reconnect if the external service says authorization has expired.
