## Free

Free is for trying Dokki and light personal work. It includes up to 3 owned workspaces, 1 GB of storage, 25 MB max file uploads, free-tier AI models, and 5 AI messages per day. When offered in the app, new users may also see a 14-day Pro trial.

## Pro

Pro is for regular individual work. It raises the workspace cap to 20, unlocks Pro-tier models, includes a 2,000-credit daily and 6,000-credit weekly AI window, raises personal storage to 100 GB, supports 500 MB files, and lets you collaborate in Team workspaces without consuming a sponsored seat.

## Max

Max plans are for heavy AI usage. Max 5x and Max 20x both include unlimited owned workspaces, max-tier model access, 2 GB file uploads, and larger AI credit windows. Max 5x includes 500 GB storage; Max 20x includes 1 TB.

## Credits and add-ons

Dokki uses credits for AI usage accounting. One credit is priced as $0.001 retail, so 1,000 credits equals $1. Available one-time credit bundles are $10 for 10,000 credits, $50 for 50,000 credits, and $200 for 200,000 credits where add-ons are enabled.

Pro and Max plans can buy add-ons. Free users cannot buy add-ons directly.

## Teams and organizations

Workspace Team billing and organization billing solve different problems:

- Workspace Team billing upgrades a specific workspace's sponsored-seat budget.
- Organization billing manages a company account context with members, organization workspaces, model controls, usage limits, audit access, per-member organization seats, and a shared AI balance.

Use Pro when one person needs more AI and storage. Use workspace Team when a specific workspace needs more Free-plan collaborators. Use an organization when several people need shared governance, billing, and AI controls.
