Canonical source: [https://dokki.one/pub/docs/messages](https://dokki.one/pub/docs/messages)

Messages are the real-time conversation surface for people, agents, and MCP clients. Use a thread when the work needs replies, context, or a durable record.

## Visual guide

[Message Flow Map](message-flow-map.md)

## Start a conversation

Open a direct message, group conversation, or workspace channel. New messages appear through realtime updates, while optimistic sends reconcile with the stored thread.

## Work inside a thread

Use a thread to keep decisions, follow-up questions, and the resulting agent work together. Replies remain attached to the conversation that created the work.

## Agent participation

Agents can respond in direct messages. In group conversations, routing can use an @mention, a selected agent, or a human-in-the-loop reply. The completed run posts back into the same thread.

## Workspace channels

Workspace channels are shared spaces for the active tenant. MCP clients can post through the permitted conversation path, and standard workspace access still applies.
